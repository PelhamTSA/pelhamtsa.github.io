Who says Vegan food can't taste amazing? Founded 2025 by two friends with a duty to bring flavorful vegan food to Pelham, HIPPIES serves up affordable, savory BBQ and sandwiches, greens, and cruelty-free spins on southern classics. With a focus on crafting a homey and welcoming atmosphere, our interior includes our very own indoor garden consisting of beautiful flowers and greenery. Creating a scenic indoor experience for our beautiful and loyal customers. If you are malnourished and feining for fern trod on down to HIPPIES!
